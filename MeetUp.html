StandUp Meetings

Efficient meetings in style

As a company you have to listen to your customers. The MeetUp is an important example of this. People kept asking us for a simple yet beautiful meeting table around which they could meet while standing upright. Together with these people we set out to design exactly that!

Why should you meet while standing

  • Meetings take less time
  • More dynamic and interactive athmosphere
  • More attentive people around the table
