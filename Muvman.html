Muvman

Sit stand stool

The flexible base and pneumatic lift make the Muvman a really active sit stand stool. It is perfect for at the MeetUp, StandUp and the Jarvis desk.

The seat is made of a plush microfiber which is not just great looking, it adds friction to keep you secure on the seat while you lean forward. The front edge of the seat is flexible, adaptable and doesn't to impede the blood circulation behind the legs. Muvman's unique anti-pressure-point-design alleviates undue pressure.
