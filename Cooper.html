Cooper Standing Desk Converter

Transforming your current setup

Do you already have a (fixed-height) desk you love... Except for the fixed height part? The Cooper Standing Desk Converter lets you keep what you love but adds the possibility to stand at it.

Cooper has everything a "desk converter" needs: stability, usable workspace, ease of use, and sustainable craftsmanship.

It doesn't have the common incremental height adjustment (not enough height options) and the front-to-back motion you see with most desk risers (takes up too much space). Instead it has a double X-frame (think ironing board) that adjusts smoothly straight up and down, while still offering a built-in keyboard platform below the main work surface. Built-in tension adjustment can handle a variety of weights (up to 15kg),

The top surface is 52 cm by 90 cm and the keyboard platform is 29 cm by 73 cm.

Finally, a little something for the earth: it comes with the signature bamboo top an environmentally-conscious and beautiful surface.
